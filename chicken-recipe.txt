Ingredients:
 - 1 chicken

Directions:
 1. Catch the chicken.
 2. Secure the chicken and slice its head over with a large knife, preferably one stroke.
 3. Remove the feathers from the chicken however you can.
 4. Remove the chicken's wings and feet.
 5. Make a seasoning mix with salt, pepper, onion powder, paprika, and rosemary.
 6. Rub the seasoning mix liberally on and under the chicken's skin.
 7. Put the chicken in a sealed bag and place in the fridge for 3 hours.
 8. Go to Popeye's, KFC or another establishment that sells chicken.
 9. Acquire your desired amount of chicken from said establishment.
 10. Enjoy.
